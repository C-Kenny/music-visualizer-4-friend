# Bundled Processing Library Licenses

These ZIPs are vendored copies of third-party Processing libraries. We bundle
them because upstream download URLs have broken over the years (404s, dead
hosts), and CI needs reproducible installs. Each was originally distributed by
its author under the license shown below.

If you are an author and would prefer we link rather than bundle, please open
an issue.

---
